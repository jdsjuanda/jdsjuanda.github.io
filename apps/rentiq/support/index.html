RENTIQ Support

For help with RENTIQ, contact support@naomifoundry.com.

Purchases And Restore

RENTIQ includes a Free tier and may offer RENTIQ Solo Lifetime and RENTIQ Portfolio Lifetime as non-consumable App Store purchases. If you bought a lifetime unlock and it is not active in the app, use Restore Purchases in RENTIQ when available and make sure you are signed in with the Apple account used for the purchase.

Refunds

Refund requests for App Store purchases are processed by Apple. View Apple's refund request instructions.

Privacy

Read the RENTIQ Privacy Policy for information about local storage, iCloud synchronization, and App Store purchases.
